Questions? Answers.

  • Why Hustleware instead of hiring?

    Hiring a full-time senior designer these days not only can take you months to find a good one available, it will also cost you $100,000 upwards, plus benefits. With Hustleware, you get instant access to a highly-qualified designer — so you can skip the hiring process and start making progress immediately. You can also pause or cancel the service at any time should your need for design work dries up. You can't do that with a full-time hire.

  • What type of jobs aren't covered?

    Custom illustrations · Infographics · Fillable or interactive PDF documents · Complex photo manipulations · Digital painting and game assets · 3D · Animations · Video editing · Web development · Prototyping · Mobile apps · Annual reports · Keynote and Powerpoint · Content writing or manual typing

  • Who will I be working with?

    You'll work with the Hustleware founder directly, me. There's no outsourcing, no freelancer, no middle man, and no extra people involved.

  • What happens after I subscribe?

    You will get a welcome email. You will also receive an invite to join a Trello board that will be created for you, where you will be able to make design requests and keep track of progress.

  • Is there a request limit?

    Once subscribed, you can add as many requests in the backlog as you like. Each will be completed one by one. Your request will be moved to the completed section in Trello once it's finished. Meanwhile, work can begin on the next request in the queue until you provide a feedback on the previous request.

  • How fast will I receive designs?

    It takes 1-3 business days on average to receive a first draft. Design projects, by nature, have a wide range of scopes. So for smaller tasks a delivery will usually mean a completed design. For complex requests, the task will be divided into milestones and delivered accordingly.

  • Who owns the rights to the designs?

    You have 100% ownership of the designs the moment they're delivered. This includes the actual design and the source files.

  • What if I don't like the design?

    No problem. Your subscription includes unlimited revisions until you're completely satisfied. But this almost never happens.

  • What design tools do you use?

    Most requests are designed using Figma. Illustrator or Photoshop in some cases. Webflow for no-code development.

  • Do you offer refunds?

    Unfortunately not. Due to the amount of research and pressure put in to produce high-quality work in a short period of time, it becomes difficult to justify going uncompensated for the time and effort.

  • What if I still have questions?

    If you have any additional questions feel free to contact hello@hustleware.com
